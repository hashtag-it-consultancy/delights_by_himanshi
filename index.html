About

There are many variations

Delights By Himanshi was found by Himanshi In a home kitchen. Who used to bake 100% egg-free vegetarian cakes for the local community who follow a strict vegetarian diet. It was a challenge to make cakes without eggs and without compromising the taste and quality. We accepted this challenge and made the unique tasty cakes that was loved by every one and can be used for Every occasion.

Cup Cakes
